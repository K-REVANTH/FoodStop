STEPS TO ORDER

  1. Select the food items by double-clicking on their respective LIKE buttons.
  2. Click on 'Your Address' button and enter your address.
  3. Click on 'Items' button. You will be directed to another page showing your order.
  4. You can adjust the quantity of items as per your requirements.
  5. Click on 'PAY' button. Your order will be placed. You can see an alert box notifying the same.
  6. Refresh the page to go to Home page
